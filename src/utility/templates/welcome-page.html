A word to the engineers

Every Sunday, people walk through the doors of a church. They register, they check in, they give, they grow. Behind every one of those moments — if that church runs {{ product_name }} — there is a request hitting this server.

You are not just writing endpoints. You are building the infrastructure that lets a congregation run smoothly, that lets a pastor know who showed up, that lets a worker follow up with a first-timer before the week is out. That is not a small thing.

Write clean migrations. Cache intentionally. Log what matters. Every line of code you write here has a person on the other end of it. Build accordingly.
